Tincat Player is a lightweight player that supports most video and music formats.

* Beautiful UI Tincat Player has a beautiful user interface, simple and elegant. Simple to use and supports night mode to protect your eyes.

* All Video & Music Formats Support Tincat Player not only has a beautiful UI, but also supports almost all video and music formats, including mp3, mp4, m3u8, flv, mpd, avi, rmvb and more.

* Gesture Tincat Player also supports excellent gestures, you can adjust video progress, brightness, volume and more with one finger. You can also zoom the video with two fingers.

* Subtitle Tincat Player also supports external subtitles. You can choose to add srt subtitles to the video for playback. If you do not have srt subtitles, Tincat Player also supports converting other formats of subtitles to srt subtitles.

* Lyric Tincat Player also supports local or online lyrics for music player.

Live Recorder is a powerful app that lets you follow, record and download live streams from your favorite platforms.

Currently supporting TikTok, Bigo, LiveMe and more platforms.

Live Recorder supports the following features:

1. Live Stream Recording & Download
    Record and download live streams in HD quality with all available resolutions. Save streams for offline viewing anytime, anywhere.

2. Follow Your Favorite Streamers
    Keep track of your favorite live streamers. Follow them and never miss their live broadcasts.

3. Author Status Monitor
    After following, you can monitor streamers' real-time status (online or offline). Get notified when they go live.

4. Multi-Platform Support
    One app for all your live streaming needs. Support TikTok, Bigo, LiveMe and more platforms.

5. Easy to Use
    Simple and intuitive interface. Just paste the live stream link and start downloading with one tap.

6. High Quality Downloads
    Download live streams in the highest quality available. Choose from multiple resolution options.
